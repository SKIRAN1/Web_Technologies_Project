Dr. Neeraj opened his clinic there in order to offer quality dental care to people in the area. Thus, with its team of dentists, dental hygienists and administrative staff, it offers a wide range of dental services. Located on Rosemount, the Hygiene Dental Center offers a calm and warm environment where patients feel good. They are also welcomed and taken care of by professionals passionate about dental practice and the overall well-being of everyone.


Come and enjoy our quality care and the excellent service offered by the members of our united and dynamic team. Whether you are already patient with us or want to join our big family, we will welcome you with enthusiasm. Thus, we will take the time to assess your situation and listen to your needs. Whether you are looking for a simple routine follow-up or if you need more specific care aimed at restoring its full function or improving the appearance of your teeth, we are here for you!
